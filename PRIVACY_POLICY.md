# Menu Quotes Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

Menu Quotes does not collect, track, transmit, or sell personal data.

## Data Handling

- Quotes you add are stored locally on your Mac.
- Import and export actions only read from or write to files you explicitly choose.
- The app does not include analytics, advertising, tracking, or third-party data collection.
- The app does not require account creation or sign-in.
